Martyrs' Day in Myanmar, known as Azani Nei, is a significant national holiday commemorated on July 19th each year. It honors the memory of General Aung San and eight other leaders of the pre-independence interim government who were assassinated on that day in 1947. These leaders played pivotal roles in Myanmar's struggle for independence from British colonial rule.
